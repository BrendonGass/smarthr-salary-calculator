Gmail Setup:
  • SMTP Server: smtp.gmail.com
  • Port: 587
  • Enable TLS
  • Use App Password (not regular password)
Office 365 Setup:
  • SMTP Server: smtp.office365.com
  • Port: 587
  • Enable TLS
  • Use your Office 365 credentials
Note: For Gmail, you'll need to enable 2-factor authentication and generate an App Password. Regular passwords won't work due to security restrictions.
